üöÄ Quick Start

  1. Open Microsoft Edge and go to edge://extensions/
  2. Enable "Developer mode"
  3. Click "Load unpacked" and select the browser-extension folder
  4. Start copying text - it will automatically be saved to your history!
  5. Click the extension icon to view and manage your clipboard history

üí° Usage Tips

  • Click any item in the history to copy it back to your clipboard
  • Use the search box to quickly find specific copied text
  • Hover over items to see Copy and Delete buttons
  • Use "Clear All" to reset your clipboard history
  • The extension works on all websites automatically
